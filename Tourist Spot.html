The coastal town of Pagudpud, located in the Philippines' northern Ilocos province,
is well-known for its gorgeous beaches, alluring natural features, and relaxed vibe.
Because of its stunning white sand beaches, it's sometimes referred to as the "Boracay of the North".
With its pure surroundings and peaceful way of life, Pagudpud has a certain charm of its own,
even though it might not have the busy nightlife and commercialism of Boracay.
